About Us

TonikuBento Resto celebrates authentic Indonesian cuisine in the best possible way with a menu that takes guests on a flavourful journey across the archipelago. The venue itself is a customized Javanese joglo designed to recreate the cultural nuance of a bygone era. Nestled within a tropical garden, TonikuBento Resto is the perfect spot for an intimate or a dine socially with family and friends.
